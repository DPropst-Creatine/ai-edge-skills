Study Shrink Workspace

Local text cleaner, chunker, prompt builder, evidence-card workspace.

What this app does

This webpage does not run an LLM by itself. It prepares study text for your AI skill or ChatGPT by cleaning, estimating token load, splitting chunks, creating prompts, and storing/exporting compact evidence cards locally.
